Fort Mifflin is located next to Philadelphia International Airport on the banks of the Delaware River and is a short drive (10-15 minutes) from anywhere in or near Center City Philadelphia.


Fort Mifflin can be easily accessed by car, taxi, Uber / Lyft, and even by bicycle !!!


You can take Uber / Lyft or taxi to Fort Mifflin when arriving to Making Time ∞ but, Uber / Lyft and taxis CANNOT pick you up at Fort Mifflin when leaving.


In an effort to provide a safer experience when leaving Making Time ∞ at Fort Mifflin, we are not allowing Uber / Lyft or taxis to pick up at the gates of Fort Mifflin.


If you choose to take Uber / Lyft or Taxi when leaving Fort Mifflin, you will be required to take our FREE shuttles to our nearby Uber / Lyft / Taxi pick-up location at ARB Parking at 4700 Island Ave.


We will be announcing more information on our Making Time ∞ Roundtrip Shuttle Bus Service from Central Philadelphia later this summer.
